RdmUnmatch Kulcsszó

To get the RdmUnmatch keyword in the command line, type RdmUnmatch vagy rövidenRdm a parancssorba.

Leírás

The RdmUnmatch keyword unmatches previous matched RDM devices in the show file. If no object is given, the current selection will be used.

Possible objects are:

  • Channel
  • Fixture
  • FixtureType
  • RDMFixtureType
  • Group
  • Sequence
  • Cue
  • Executor
  • Preset (only selective Presets)
  • Selection
  • World

The RdmUnmatch keyword egy függvény kulcsszó.

Szintaxis

RDMUnmatch [Objektum-lista]

Példák

  • To unmatch the selected fixtures, írd be a parancssorba:
[Channel]> RdmUnmatch

 

  • To unmatch the fixtures used in group 1, írd be a parancssorba:
[Channel]> RdmUnmatch Group 1

 

  • To unmatch the fixtures used in group 1 and in sequence 2, írd be a parancssorba:
[Channel]> RdmUnmatch Group 1 If Sequence 2
