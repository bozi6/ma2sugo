MAtricksWings Kulcsszó

To get the MAtricksWings kulcsszó beírásához, írd be MAtricksWings or one of the shortcuts MW or MAtricksW a parancssorba.

Leírás

MAtricksWings egy függvény kulcsszó to set the MAtricks Wings value.

With the MAtricksWings the fixture selection is splitted in x parts and each part is mirroring the previous part.

For more information see MAtricks.

Szintaxis

MAtricksWings [parts]

MAtricksWings + | -

MAtricksWings Off

Példa

To divide the fixture selection in two parts and the second part mirrors the adjusted values írd be a parancssorba:

[Channel]> MatricksWings 2
